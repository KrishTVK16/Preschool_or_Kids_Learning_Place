Admissions

Start your child's educational journey with us

Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to enroll your child at Bright Beginnings Preschool

Step 1: Schedule a Tour

Visit our facility, meet our teachers, and see our programs in action. Tours are available Monday through Friday.

Step 2: Submit Application

Complete our enrollment application form with your child's information and program preferences.

Step 3: Review & Acceptance

We'll review your application and notify you of acceptance within 5 business days.

Step 4: Enrollment

Complete enrollment paperwork, submit required documents, and pay registration fees.

Enrollment Form

Required Documents

Please prepare the following documents for enrollment:

  • Birth certificate or passport
  • Immunization records
  • Emergency contact information
  • Medical information and allergies
  • Parent/guardian ID
  • Proof of address
  • Enrollment agreement
  • Registration fee payment
