Frequently Asked Questions

We are open Monday through Friday from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and major holidays.

We serve children from 18 months to 12 years old, with programs for toddlers (18 months - 2.5 years), preschoolers (2.5 - 4 years), pre-K (4 - 5 years), and after-school care (5 - 12 years).

You can schedule a tour by calling us at (555) 123-4567, emailing admissions@brightbeginnings.com, or filling out the contact form above. We offer tours Monday through Friday between 9:00 AM and 4:00 PM.

Our ratios vary by program: Toddler Program (1:4), Preschool Program (1:6), Pre-K Program (1:8), and After School (1:10). These ratios exceed state requirements to ensure individualized attention.

Yes, we provide nutritious morning and afternoon snacks. Parents can choose to pack lunch or purchase our hot lunch program. All meals follow nutritional guidelines and accommodate dietary restrictions.
